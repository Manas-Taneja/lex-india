# Disability Rights

## Definition
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act 2016 (RPwD Act) replaced the Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Protection of Rights and Full Participation) Act 1995 and expanded the list of recognised disabilities from 7 to 21, bringing Indian law into conformity with the UN Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (CRPD).

## Current Law

### Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act 2016
- § 2(s) — "Person with benchmark disability": a person with ≥40% of specified disability as certified by a certifying authority
- § 3 — Right to equality and non-discrimination: no discrimination on basis of disability; reasonable accommodation to be provided
- § 16 — Inclusive education: government to ensure free education for children with disabilities up to 18 years; 3% seats reserved in govt educational institutions
- § 23 — Equal opportunity policy: every establishment with ≥20 employees to publish and implement an equal opportunity policy
- § 24 — Non-discrimination in employment: no establishment to discriminate against persons with disabilities in recruitment, promotion, or other employment matters
- § 25 — Health care: persons with disabilities entitled to free healthcare in proximity to their communities
- § 34 — Reservation in government posts: not less than 4% of vacancies in central government posts reserved for persons with benchmark disabilities (distributed across categories)
- § 38 — Special Employment Exchange: state governments to maintain special employment exchanges for persons with disabilities
- § 40 — Accessibility in public buildings: all public buildings to comply with accessibility standards within prescribed timelines
- § 41 — Accessibility in transport: all transport systems to comply with accessibility standards
- § 42 — Accessibility in information and communication: information in accessible formats (Braille, sign language, easy language)
- §§ 76–88 — Offences and penalties: contravention punishable with fine (up to ₹10,000 first offence; ₹50,000–₹5 lakh for repeat)

### Rights of Persons with Disabilities Rules 2017
- Procedure for issuing Disability Certificate and Unique Disability ID (UDID)
- Composition and functions of District Disability Rehabilitation Centre (DDRC)
- Format of equal opportunity policy

## Key Provisions

### 21 Specified Disabilities (Schedule)
| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| Physical | Locomotor disability, leprosy cured, dwarfism, acid attack victims |
| Visual | Blindness, low vision |
| Hearing | Deaf, hard of hearing, deaf-blindness |
| Speech | Speech and language disability |
| Intellectual | Intellectual disability, specific learning disability |
| Mental | Mental illness, autism spectrum disorder |
| Multiple/others | Cerebral palsy, muscular dystrophy, chronic neurological conditions, thalassemia, haemophilia, sickle cell disease, multiple sclerosis |

### Reservation in Central Government Employment (§ 34)
| Category | Reserved Percentage |
|----------|-------------------|
| Blindness and low vision | 1% |
| Deaf and hard of hearing | 1% |
| Locomotor disability including cerebral palsy, leprosy cured, dwarfism, acid attack victims | 1% |
| Autism, intellectual disability, specific learning disability, mental illness | 1% |
| **Total** | **4%** |

### Grievance Redressal Mechanism
| Authority | Level | Function |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| Grievance Redressal Officer | Establishment | First point of redressal within establishments |
| District Disability Rehabilitation Centre (DDRC) | District | Disability certification, rehabilitation services |
| State Commissioner for Persons with Disabilities | State | Complaints, inquiries, monitoring |
| Chief Commissioner for Persons with Disabilities | Central | Coordination, safeguarding rights, annual report |

### Unique Disability ID (UDID)
- Single document serving as proof of disability and identity nationwide
- Issued by Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment through DDRC
- Linked to disability certificate; eliminates need to obtain fresh certificate for each benefit
