# Provident Fund

## Definition
The Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) is a mandatory retirement savings scheme administered by the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) under the EPF & MP Act 1952, requiring joint contributions from employees and employers to build a corpus for retirement, disability, and death benefits.

## Current Law

### Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act 1952

- § 1(3) — applicability: every establishment employing ≥20 persons in any industry specified in Schedule I; voluntary coverage possible for smaller establishments
- § 2(f) — "employee": any person employed for wages in any kind of work (manual, clerical, supervisory, technical) directly or through contractor
- § 5 — EPF Scheme 1952: framed thereunder; governs contributions, accounts, nominations, withdrawals
- § 6 — contribution: employee contributes 12% of basic wages + DA + retaining allowance; employer contributes equal 12%
- § 6A — Employees' Pension Scheme (EPS) 1995: 8.33% of employer's contribution (on wage capped at ₹15,000/month) diverted to EPS; balance 3.67% goes to EPF account
- § 6C — Employees' Deposit Linked Insurance (EDLI) Scheme 1976: employer contributes 0.5% of wages (max ₹75 lakh insurance cover on death in service)
- § 7A — determination of money due: EPFO Commissioner may determine dues; employer must pay
- § 7Q — interest on delayed payment: 12% per annum
- § 14B — damages for default: EPFO may levy damages 5%–25% of arrears depending on delay period
- § 17 — exemption: establishments with own approved provident fund may be exempted

## Key Provisions

### Contribution Breakdown

| Contribution | Rate | Destination |
|--------------|------|-------------|
| Employee contribution | 12% of basic + DA | EPF account |
| Employer — EPF portion | 3.67% of basic + DA | EPF account |
| Employer — EPS portion | 8.33% of basic + DA (capped ₹15,000) | EPS 1995 |
| Employer — EDLI | 0.5% of basic + DA | EDLI Scheme 1976 |
| Admin charges | 0.5% (employer) | EPFO administration |

### Withdrawal Rules

| Circumstance | Eligibility |
|--------------|-------------|
| Retirement / 58 years | Full EPF + EPS (as monthly pension) |
| Unemployment >2 months | 75% of EPF corpus after 1 month; 100% after 2 months |
| Marriage / education | Up to 50% of own contribution after 7 years |
| Medical treatment | Up to 6× monthly wages or own contribution, whichever less |
| Housing (purchase/construction) | Up to 90% of corpus after 5 years |
| Death in service | Full corpus to nominee; EDLI insurance claim |

### Universal Account Number (UAN)
- Unique 12-digit number allotted by EPFO to every member
- Portable across employers; employee retains same UAN throughout career
- Enables online passbook access, KYC updates, and online claims

### EPS 1995 Pension

| Condition | Benefit |
|-----------|---------|
| Minimum service for pension | 10 years vesting |
| Pension commencement age | 58 years (reduced pension from 50) |
| Formula | Monthly pension = (Pensionable salary × Pensionable service) / 70 |
| Maximum pensionable salary (currently) | ₹15,000/month (subject to Supreme Court directions on higher pension) |
